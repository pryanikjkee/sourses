Product approval & certification

BBA certification is recognised throughout the construction industry as a symbol of quality and reassurance. It's a vital ingredient for product manufacturers to help ensure acceptance with specifiers, building control.

  • Agrement certificates
  • Windows and doors certification
  • Microgeneration certification scheme (MCS)
  • Highway authorities product approval (HAPAS)
  • CE Marking
  • Environmental certification
  • Competency Scheme
  • European Technical Assessments
  • Factory production control

BBA experts can also assess, audit and test during the development of a manufacturer’s product. The resultant impartial reports provided by the BBA can help manufacturers make judgements on how to proceed with the development and these reports can be used as supporting evidence if applying for full Agrément Approval.

Products that receive Agrément Certificates are recognised by building control, government departments, architects, specifiers and industry insurers. It’s a mark of quality, safety and reliability that provides reassurance of the product’s fitness-for-purpose. For new construction products it is vital to achieve this certification if they are to gain a quick route to the marketplace.

Once the certificate has been awarded, regular monitoring is carried out by the BBA to maintain the Certificate’s validity. This is followed by a comprehensive review every three years.
